About Me

I'm a passionate data enthusiast. I have dedicated my time in uncovering insights in data to help make smarter business decisions. I take pride in playing the behind-the-scenes rolesof a data analyst through collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data from common xlsx, csv, json, sql files, identifying patterns and trends, interpreting statistical results, creating visualizations to present insights, generating reports, and communicating findings to stakeholders to inform business decisions.
My journey into data analytics began in 2022 when I started using Microsoft Excel to analyze student performance. I faced challenges with limitations, and curiosity led me to discover SQL and Python—tools that transformed how I work with data. Teaching myself daily, I unlocked new possibilities, enhancing efficiency and delivering deeper insights.
